Overview

Purpose

Looking for a thrilling adventure on the river? Big Adventure Rafting is commited to showing our guests the beauty, excitement, and experience of whitewater rafting.

Audience

Are you an expert on the river? Perhaps you have some experience, but could use a refresher course? Or maybe this will be your very first time whitewater rafting? Whatever your level of experience may be, Big Adventure Rafting has trained professionals who are knowledgeable of the river and can match just the right section of the river to fill your thrill. We have sections of the river for kids as young as 5 years old, where they can enjoy the spashy waves, on-raft games and silly guide stories. We have big time adventurous tours that are sure to give you a ride of a lifetime and also everything in between.
