Washington

Washington is known as the Evergreen State because of its year-round rainfall. The consistent rain maintains a beautifully green and natural scene state wide. It is also home to the famous Seattle Space Needle, one of the largest and tallest tourist attractions in the U.S. There are many National Parks throughout the State. It sits just below British Columbia, Canada opening easy access to many of treasures in Vancouver, BC.
